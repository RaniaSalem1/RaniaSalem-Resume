Hello, my name is Rania Salem. I am from the Bay Area, San Francisco to be specific. I am a second year political science with a focus in international affairs and ethnic studies major at UC Riverside. Before UCR I attended Balboa high school in San Francisco. I currently work at UC Riverside, Student Recreation Center Cooking Well Department as a cooking assistant. At my current position I help out with the cooking demonstrations that take place every week at the Student Recreation Center . In preparation for the demos, I go grocery shopping for all the ingredients that the participants need. Once we have all the needed ingredients, we portion and prepare them before the demos. During the demos, I help the participants cook their dishes and assist the chef in teaching the participants. After demos, I have to clean the kitchen, do the dishes, take out the trash, and lock up. This is one of my many job experiences. Last school year I worked at the Subway on campus. At Subway, I mostly prepared sandwiches and salads. However, I also did prep in the mornings, where I cut veggies and portioned out the proteins. I also had the opportunity of cashiering. I have also had other jobs in the food industry. This includes Jamba Juice and Chipotle. I have also worked in retail, this includes Target and a local store in San Francisco called 12 Small Things. In addition, I have experience working within the heart of the Bay Area community. I have worked at after school programs, recreation centers, and community agencies as a counselor in training.
